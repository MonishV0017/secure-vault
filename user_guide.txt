SecureVault User Guide

Welcome to Secure Vault! This guide explains how to use all the features of the application.

-----------------------------
1. Account Management
-----------------------------
    
Creating an Account:
  - Click the "Sign Up" button on the main screen.
  - Enter a unique username, email address, and a "Strong" password (the app will guide you).
  - Enter the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your email to verify it.
  - Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (like Google Authenticator) to set up Two-Factor Authentication (MFA).

Logging In:
  - You will need your Username, Password, the on-screen CAPTCHA, and your 6-digit MFA code from your authenticator app.

Forgot Password:
  - This feature helps you reset your password if you are logged out. It requires access to your registered email.
  - The success message will also remind you of your username.

Change Password:
  - This button appears on the main dashboard after you log in.
  - It allows you to change your login password, but you must know your current password to do so.

-----------------------------
2. Core Vault Features
-----------------------------

Uploading a File:
  - Click "Upload File to Encrypt".
  - Select a file from your computer.
  - WARNING: You must enter a strong passphrase for each file. Do not forget this passphrase, as it is the only way to decrypt your file.

Listing Files:
  - Shows a complete, searchable list of all your encrypted files. You can type in the search bar to filter the list.

Deleting Files:
  - Click "Delete Encrypted Files".
  - Select one or more files using the checkboxes.
  - WARNING: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Retrieving a File:
  - Click "Retrieve File".
  - Select a single file using the radio button.
  - You must enter the correct, original passphrase you used when you uploaded that specific file.
  - Choose a location on your computer to save the decrypted file. A success message will give you an "Open File" button for convenience.

-----------------------------
3. Data Safety & Admin
-----------------------------

Backup Vault:
  - Creates a single, secure backup file of your current vault.
  - WARNING: You will be asked for a GPG Password to encrypt the entire backup. Do not lose this password.
  - It is highly recommended to store backup files on an external drive.

Restore Vault:
  - Restores your vault from a backup file.
  - WARNING: This will completely delete all files currently in your vault and replace them with the files from the backup.
  - You can ONLY restore backups that belong to your specific user account.

View Activity Log:
  - Shows a complete history of all actions on your account. Use this to check for suspicious activity.

Delete Account:
  - Permanently deletes your account and all associated files.
  - WARNING: This is irreversible. Your existing backups will become unusable.

Factory Reset App (Login Screen):
  - The most destructive action. It completely WIPES the entire application for ALL users.
  - It is protected by the admin. Please contact the admin email, setup by the distributor/developer who had shared the .exe file for further details.
